Banana Bread

Our most popular banana bread is moist, delicious, and absolutely packed with banana flavor.
This Allrecipes community favorite will quickly become your go-to banana bread recipe!

ingredient

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas
  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
